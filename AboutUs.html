We are a Jabalpur-based design and
brand strategy agency.

The goal of Artcetra , founded by Samriddhi Nagdev, Creative Director, is to
develop a new wave of challenger businesses in India. She wants to
completely transform the industry by working with highly talented creatives
and driven brand leaders.

"Entrepreneurs transforming India's consumer brand landscape excite me greatly.
These entrepreneurs have a lot to negotiate in the complicated country of India. I take
pleasure in closely collaborating with creators and leadership teams to reimagine the
potential and possibilities for brands in India."

Our Services

BRAND STRATEGY
We assist you in discovering your brand's mission and core values. What sets it apart and keeps it relevant in the modern world. We accomplish this by developing a purpose for your brand and locating pertinent customer information.
NAMING
"Universally, irrespective of the product, range, or even your primary brand name."
VISUAL IDENTITY
Using Photoshop Illustrator, Canva, Figma, designs, and wordmarks to give your brand a face.
VERBAL IDENTITY
A visual identity is no longer sufficient to establish a brand in the modern world...
CREATIVE DIRECTION
Your brand's influence might be positively or negatively impacted by the way your graphics and images feel. The final impression that customers receive of the brand depends greatly on how these assets are set up.
BRAND COMMUNICATION
DESIGN
Since every brand is unique, different elements are needed to bring it to life. We can assist you with the design of any kind of asset, from the aesthetics of your Instagram posts to the appearance of a web banner featuring your business.
PACKAGING & UNBOXING
For your consumer-facing packaging, we design a distinctive style that's successful equally online and offline. The method by which your brand has been developed to engage clients right away all through the unboxing experience has become another essential touchpoint for directly to the consumer promotion.
DESIGN FOR WEB
Designing the aesthetic and feel for the online presence of your business through your website. For a few high impact screens on your website, we generally establish the style and tone. Development agencies may discover this very valuable when they enhance their digital presence. We might additionally collaborate with you to put together the key elements of your digital visual assets, that includes the copy, graphic design, snapshots, and iconography.
ANIMATED VIDEOS
Even though we rarely do it frequently, we enjoy coming up with compelling stories and taking complex data and producing unambiguous, effortless animated movies that accentuate a point or tell an account.
BRAND GUIDELINES
For brands with numerous diverse touch-points to be documented, we layout significantly bigger dockets; for your brand, we build guidelines that could be useful as a starting point.
BRAND MENTORING
 An experimental sort of collaboration whereby we engage you for a discussion and generate ideas for a certain problem statement. This is very adaptable, and we appreciate to work with brands that are interested in working with us on one brief endeavour first.
